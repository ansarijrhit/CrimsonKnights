Draveik Deck 2.0

Draveik: 35 HP, You always have +1 attack and AC

Attunement:
- Roaring Deliverance: After Draveik has taken 3 attacks, equip this.
- Duesiderre: Spend an action to discard a card and lose 3 HP, add 1 counter to this weapon. Once you have reached 4 counters, equip this weapon.
- Retribution Spine: After Draveik has received a total of 12 damage on your turns, equip this.

Weapons:
- Roaring Deliverance: +1 attack and when you hit but don't destroy a companion, send it back to that opponent's hand.
- Duesiderre: +4 attack

Equipment:
- Retribution Spine: +1 AC, Any enemy that attacks your hero directly takes 1d4 damage.


Other Cards:
(Companions)
- 1x Daennis:(Companion) has +2 attack and +5 HP. Whenever your hero attacks, draw a card, then you can discard one.
- 1x Sealed Dielong/ Unsealed Dielong:(Companion)(Duo-Discard) has +1 attack and +8 HP/ has +6 attack and +2 HP, you can take 5 damage to summon this when discarded.
- 1x Winter:(Companion) has +2 attack and +3 HP. After you end your turn, draw a card.
(Spells)
- 1x Absorb Elements:(Spell)(Reaction) When your hero is dealt damage, roll 1d6 and subtract from that instance of damage. On your hero's next attack, add 1d6 damage to it.
- 1x Zephyr Strike:(Spell) Draw a card, your hero's next attack this turn deals an extra 1d4 damage.
(Abilities)
- 1x Action Surge:(Ability) You get two additional actions during this action phase, draw a card.
- 1x Draveik's Fire Breath:(Ability) Roll 1d6, deal that much damage to all enemies.
- 3x Extra Attack:(Ability) Your hero can make two attacks instead of one during this combat phase.
- 1x Last Ditch Effort:(Ability) Deal 10 to your hero and then if you are alive, deal 10 to 1 other hero and that opponent's companions.
- 1x Quality Build:(Ability) Until the start of your next turn, your hero's attack and AC is equal to their highest of the two.
- 1x Second Wind:(Ability) Heal 1d10 HP to your hero.
- 1x Slayers Prey:(Ability) Choose an enemy, your next attacks against them in a turn deals an extra 1d6 of damage.
(Reactions)
- 1x Indomitable: (Reaction) When a player targets you with a card, put it back in there hand and make them play another card in their hand if able.
(Duo-Discards)
- 1x Blind Vengeance/ Pure Intent:(Ability) Discard 1 card and draw 2./ (Discarded) Draw 1 card.
- 2x Feinting/ Precision Attack:(Ability) Discard another card, your hero's next attack deals 1d6 more damage and can reroll any rolled damage by your hero once this turn only./ (Discarded) Your next attack ignores AC.
- 2x Goading/ Grappling Strike:(Ability) Until the start of your next turn, gain +2 attack and every time you are damaged, discard a card and gains another 2 attack./ (Discarded) Each opponent you attack next skips their draw phase.
- 1x Know Your Enemy/ Favored Foe:(Ability) Choose an opponent and look into their hand, then discard one of your own cards. Your opponent must discard a card of the same type if possible./ (Discarded) Choose an opponent, until the start of your next turn whenever they play a card, draw a card, then you may discard one.
- 1x Land Strider/Deft Explorer:(Ability) Take two cards from your discard pile and shuffle them into your deck, then draw one./ (Discarded) Draw 2 cards, then shuffle one card from your discard pile into your deck.
- 2x Pushing Attack/ Sweeping Strike:(Ability) Discard a card, your opponent must put one card in their hand on top of their deck and one of their cards on the field into their hand if possible./ (Discarded) When you attack with your hero, deal that damage to an additionally opponent or enemy minion of your choice.
- 1x Strong Will/ Conflicting Ideals:(Ability) Discard any amount of cards from your hand, your hero gains 2 attack + 1 more for each card discarded this turn./ (Discarded) The next time you are attacked, deal 3 additional retaliation damage.
